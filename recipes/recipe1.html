Greek chichen skewers

Description

Greek-inspired chicken skewers with lots of flavor!

Marinade is also wonderful on grilled vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup wok oil
  • 1/8 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon onion flakes
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 1 teaspoon Greek seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 3 skinless, boneless, chicken breasts, or as needed, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 8 bamboo skewers, or as needed, soaked in water for 30 minutes
    •

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Whisk lemon juice, oil, vinegar, onion flakes, garlic, lemon zest, Greek seasoning, poultry seasoning, oregano, pepper, and thyme together in a bowl and pour into a resealable plastic bag.

  2. Step 2

    3. Add chicken, coat with the marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.

  3. Step 3

    4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  4. Step 4

    5. Remove chicken from the marinade and shake off excess; thread onto skewers. Discard the remaining marinade. Place the skewers on a baking sheet.

  5. Step 5

    6. Roast in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 20 minutes.
